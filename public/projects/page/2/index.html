NFC Pen Testing GLove

This was a relatively small and quick project aimed to expose the security risks of NFC tap access doors used at most all universities. I began learning how RFID cards work when I found a Proxmark 3 Easy in a maker space, and went down a deep rabbit hole of trying to read the data on every card that I owned. I had great fun doing this and learning about the many different RFID card types. I now collect tap cards like they are pokemon cards. ...
